Time Complexity: O(n*m), where n is the length of the A sequence and m the length of the B sequence.
Additional Space: O(n*m)

PSEUDOCODE

(Recurrence relation)
lcs[i][0]=0, i=1,...,n
lcs[0][j]=0, j=1,...,m
            | lcs[i-1][j-1],                 if A[i]==B[i]
lcs[i][j] = | max(lcs[i-1][j], lcs[i][j-1],  if A[i]!=B[i]
